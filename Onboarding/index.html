Once approved, guests can discover and book your listing. You'll be notified of each booking via email.

Listing Requirements
  • Accurate address and property description
  • At least 3 clear, well-lit photos
  • Correct pricing in RWF
  • Accurate availability calendar
  • No misleading or false claims
Tips for Success
  • Respond to booking inquiries within 12 hours
  • Keep your availability up to date
  • Upload high-quality, natural-light photos
  • List all amenities accurately
  • Competitive pricing gets more bookings

How Payouts Work

AfriStay charges a small platform service fee per confirmed booking. You earn the booking total minus this fee. Payouts are processed after a guest's check-in and coordinated through the AfriStay team. Contact info@afristay.rw for payout details specific to your account.
